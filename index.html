Our Mission

GlobeFarms Venture Agritech Ltd was born from a simple conviction: that Africa's greatest agricultural challenges are also her greatest opportunities. Founded by a 20-year-old Engineer, we are proving that youth, technology, and ambition can transform food systems at scale.

Operating across 310+ hectares of smart farmland in Nigeria — with a freshly allocated 10 hectares from the Osun State Government — we integrate solar-powered irrigation, IoT sensor networks, and data-driven crop management into every field we touch.

The Smart Lab

A Living
Laboratory
for the Future

Our Founder's background in Electrical & Electronics Engineering isn't incidental — it's the engine of our technological edge. Every hectare is instrumented with sensors, every irrigation decision is data-driven, and every farm is a classroom.

The GlobeFarms Smart Lab is where precision agriculture meets African ingenuity: a demonstration site that trains, tests, and transfers technology to smallholder farmers at scale.

Engineering-Led Design
EEE undergraduate expertise applied directly to field systems
Replicable at Scale
Designed for modular deployment across Nigeria and West Africa

Solar Irrigation

Off-grid solar-powered pump systems ensure 24/7 water availability without diesel dependency — reducing operational costs by up to 60% while cutting carbon footprint.

Deployed on Pilot Plots

Farm Data Dashboards

Real-time IoT sensor networks monitor soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and crop health. Aggregated on cloud dashboards accessible via mobile — giving farmers actionable intelligence, not just data.

IoT + Cloud Analytics

Precision Farming

Variable-rate application of inputs (fertilizer, water, pesticides) guided by GPS field mapping and satellite imagery analysis. Less waste, higher yields, better margins for every farmer.

GPS + Satellite Intelligence

AgriTech Training Hub

The Smart Lab doubles as a live classroom. 6,500+ youth per year experience hands-on training with real equipment, real sensors, and real farms — making the learning irreversible.
